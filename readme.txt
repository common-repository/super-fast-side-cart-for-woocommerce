== Description ==

###  Super Fast Side Cart for WooCommerce

Give your customers the best shopping experience with a super fast slide-in floating side cart. Completely Ajax based so no need to refresh the page. A side cart will help your customers access the cart quickly and easily, thus improving their experience of your WooCommerce Store.


=  Features Included: =


-Custom Cart Text
-Custom Button Text
-Show/Hide Product Name
-Show/Hide Product Image
-Show/Hide Product Variations
-Show/Hide Product Price
-Show/Hide Cart Total
-Show/Hide Product Link
-Auto Open Cart on/off
-Mobile ShowHide Cart Icon
-Mobile Friendly
-Add/change side cart icon


== Frequently Asked Questions ==
Q: How Do I Set It up?
A: Install the plugin, Activate and click “Enable Side Cart” in the Side Cart settings - simple!

Q:Can I show Cross Sells on the plugin?
A:This feature is coming soon.
